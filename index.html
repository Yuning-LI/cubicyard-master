Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate cubic yards manually?
To calculate cubic yards, you first need to convert all your measurements (length, width, and depth) into feet. Multiply them together to get the total cubic feet, and then divide that number by 27.
Formula: (L × W × H) ÷ 27
How many bags of concrete make a cubic yard?
It depends on the size of the bag. Generally, you need:
  • 45 bags of 80lb concrete mix
  • 60 bags of 60lb concrete mix
  • 90 bags of 40lb concrete mix
Always order 5-10% extra for spillage or uneven ground.
How much area does 1 cubic yard of mulch cover?
Coverage depends on depth. One cubic yard covers:
  • 324 sq. ft. at 1 inch deep
  • 162 sq. ft. at 2 inches deep
  • 108 sq. ft. at 3 inches deep (Recommended for weed control)
